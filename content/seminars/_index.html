WED Lab Online Training Seminar (Webinar)

"Women’s Economic Empowerment: What We Measure And Why It Matters"
April 17, 2019, 1:00-3:00pm (EST)

This is the first in a series of seven research-to-practice training seminars on women’s empowerment organized by the WED Lab in partnership with Canada’s International Development Research Centre.

This 2-hour webinar will discuss state of the art research on measurement of women’s economic empowerment. A diverse panel of experts from the worlds of research, policy and practice will discuss contemporary challenges, opportunities and approaches for measuring this complex concept.

Featured panelists will include: Sonia Laszlo (McGill University), Arjan de Haan (International Development Research Centre), Francois-Philippe Dubé (Global Affairs Canada), and Lindsay Mossman (Aga Khan Foundation Canada).

Registration is required for this event.
